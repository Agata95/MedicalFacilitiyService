About Hospital. About Saint Joseph.

Saint Joseph Hospital is a 433-bed hospital located in Lexington, Kentucky. Founded in 1877, Saint Joseph Hospital was Lexingtonâ€™s first hospital and serves residents of central and eastern Kentucky.

At Saint Joseph Hospital, we are committed to providing the highest quality of care and are guided by our Mission and Core Values of Reverence, Integrity, Compassion and Excellence.

Also known as Lexington's "heart hospital", Saint Joseph has pioneered many firsts in the health care community. Saint Joseph has also been nationally recognized for treatment in the areas of cardiology, orthopedics and stroke.

Professionalism, safety. Modernity.

Patients of our new hospital receive top quality services guaranteed by the Saint Joseph Hospital. The medical staff of the hospital was chosen among reputable specialists with long-term experience in their specialization: surgeons, orthopaedists, gynaecologists, laryngologists - who mostly have cooperated with the Saint Joseph Hospital for many years. The Patients are well taken care of by experienced anaesthetists and nurses. The Scientific Council of the Saint Joseph Hospital will provide factual support. We guarantee our Patients the maximum safety thanks to the modern equipment of the operating rooms and the professional care.

Each Patient is treated individually. Uniqueness.

We are aware of the fact that for the full and quick recovery, besides the top quality diagnosis and treatment, it is extremely important to develop a good relationship with Patients and that is why each one of them is treated uniquely. The date of the medical intervention will be fixed individually by the doctor together with the Patient. It will be possible then to choose the most convenient date. We have set up a separate info line specifically for the Patients of the new hospital. The hospital has single and double rooms, both types comfortably equipped with individual bathrooms, TV sets and internet connection.

The Patients can be assisted by their families throughout the entire stay at the hospital. Children can be accompanied by their parents even into the operating room until the moment of narcosis, in order to reduce stress.

During their stay at the hospital the Patients can use the free-of-charge parking lot.
